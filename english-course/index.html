Welcome to Renan the Teacher's English Course!

Here you will find materials organized by Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) levels.

The CEFR is a system that helps English students track their progress and set clear learning goals, promotes transparency and comparability across different certifications, and also aids employers and educational institutions in evaluating language skills.

Enjoy the ride!
